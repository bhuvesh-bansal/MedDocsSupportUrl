Welcome to the MedDocs support page. Here you'll find information about how to use the various features of our app to manage your medical information efficiently and securely.

Appointments

The Appointments feature allows you to manage your medical appointments effectively.

How to use:

  • Create an appointment: Tap the "+" button on the Appointments screen and fill in the details (doctor, date, time, notes).
  • View appointments: All upcoming appointments are displayed on the Appointments screen in chronological order.
  • Update an appointment: Tap on an existing appointment and select "Edit" to modify any details.
  • Delete an appointment: Swipe left on an appointment or tap on it and select "Delete".
  • Set reminders: Toggle the reminder option and select how long before the appointment you want to be notified.
Tip: Set up reminders when creating appointments to receive notifications before your scheduled time. You can customize notification settings in your profile.

Medications

Keep track of all your medications, dosages, and schedules in one place.

How to use:

  • Add medication: Tap the "+" button on the Medications screen and enter medication name, dosage, frequency, and other details.
  • View medications: All your medications are listed on the Medications screen with dosage and schedule information.
  • Update medication: Tap on a medication entry and select "Edit" to update any information.
  • Delete medication: Swipe left on a medication or tap on it and select "Delete".
  • Mark as taken: Tap the checkbox next to a medication to mark your dose as taken.
  • View medication history: Tap on a medication and select "View History" to see when doses were taken.
Tip: Use the schedule feature to set reminders for taking your medications at the right times. The app can send notifications based on your medication schedule.

Reports

Store and organize all your medical reports, test results, and prescriptions in one secure location.

How to use:

  • Add a report: Tap the "+" button on the Reports screen, enter report details, and upload images of your reports or prescriptions.
  • View reports: All your reports are displayed on the Reports screen, organized by date or category.
  • Update report details: Tap on a report and select "Edit" to modify information or add additional images.
  • Delete report: Swipe left on a report or tap on it and select "Delete".
  • Upload images: When creating or editing a report, tap "Upload Image" to take a photo or select images from your gallery.
  • Share reports: Select a report and tap the "Share" button to securely share with healthcare providers.
Tip: Use the search function to quickly find specific reports by name, date, or doctor. You can also use filters to sort reports by category or date range.

Profile

Manage your personal information and app settings.

How to use:

  • View profile: Tap on the Profile icon to see your personal information.
  • Edit profile: Tap "Edit" to update your name, contact information, or emergency contacts.
  • Privacy settings: Manage your data sharing and privacy preferences.
  • Notifications: Customize which notifications you receive and how you receive them.
  • Account settings: Manage account-related options including password changes and account security.
  • Data backup: Configure automatic backups of your data to your preferred cloud storage.
Tip: We recommend adding at least one emergency contact who can be quickly contacted in case of medical emergencies.

Health Metrics

Track important health metrics over time to monitor your wellbeing.

How to use:

  • Add metrics: Tap the "+" button to add new health metrics like blood pressure, glucose levels, weight, etc.
  • View trends: See graphs and charts showing how your metrics change over time.
  • Set goals: Set target values for each metric and track your progress.
  • Export data: Export your health data in various formats to share with healthcare providers.
Tip: Regular tracking provides valuable insights for your healthcare provider. Consider setting a daily reminder to record important metrics.
